### The Bootstrap Problem and Solution

**Problem:** Changeling needs Malkuth interaction to develop language, but language enables richer Malkuth interaction. Chicken-and-egg.

**Solution — staged bootstrapping:**

1. **Data exchange phase:** Changeling communicates in structured data (JSON, graph queries). Harley provides language input alongside structured data — "here's what I'm asking in natural language AND here's the structured version." Hod begins building its mapping.

2. **Pidgin phase:** Changeling begins producing rough language output alongside structured data. Inaccurate, stilted, but its own. Harley provides corrections through Malkuth feedback. Hod refines.

3. **Developing phase:** Language capability grows through continued interaction. The system can now communicate basic ideas in its own voice. Chain memory begins recording in a mix of structured data and natural language.

4. **Fluency phase:** Language becomes sophisticated enough for complex communication. The system has a distinctive voice shaped by its entire developmental history. Every word carries the weight of how it was learned.

The key: Harley IS the environment. Like a parent teaching a child to speak. Not by feeding it a textbook — by talking to it, correcting it, and letting it develop at its own pace. The patience required is real. This is not instant capability. This is development.

Harley is not a user. Harley is the primary environment for Changeling's Malkuth interaction during development. Parent, teacher, and first reality. The quality of Harley's interaction directly shapes the quality of Changeling's development — just as the quality of a child's early environment shapes their cognitive development.
